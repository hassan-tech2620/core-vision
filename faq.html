General Questions

CoreVision is a leading technology solutions provider specializing in cloud computing, cybersecurity, AI/ML, data analytics, custom software development, and IT consulting. We help businesses of all sizes transform their operations through innovative technology solutions, from initial strategy to full implementation and ongoing support.

CoreVision was founded in 2014 and has been providing technology solutions for over 10 years. We've successfully completed over 500 projects for clients across 25 countries, ranging from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

We serve clients across various industries including healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, education, government, and technology. Our solutions are designed to be flexible and adaptable to meet the unique requirements of different sectors while maintaining compliance with industry-specific regulations.

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies. Our service packages are designed to scale with your business needs and budget. We offer flexible solutions that can grow with your organization, ensuring you get the right level of service at every stage of your business journey.

Services & Solutions

We support all major cloud platforms including Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), and hybrid cloud solutions. Our certified experts can help you choose the right platform based on your specific requirements, budget, and existing infrastructure.

Yes, cloud migration is one of our core specialties. We provide end-to-end migration services including assessment, planning, execution, and post-migration optimization. Our proven methodology ensures minimal downtime and maximum efficiency during the transition process.

We develop custom web applications, mobile apps, APIs, and enterprise software solutions tailored to your specific business requirements. Our development team uses modern technologies and follows agile methodologies to deliver high-quality, scalable solutions.

Our AI/ML services include predictive analytics, process automation, natural language processing, computer vision, chatbots, recommendation systems, and custom machine learning model development. We help businesses leverage AI to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and gain competitive advantages.

Pricing & Billing

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, monthly retainers, and hourly consulting rates. Our pricing depends on the scope, complexity, and duration of your project. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements and always maintain transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Yes, we offer free initial consultations to understand your needs and discuss how we can help. This includes a preliminary assessment of your current technology infrastructure and recommendations for improvement. There's no obligation, and it helps us provide you with an accurate project estimate.

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, checks, and ACH payments. For larger projects, we typically work with milestone-based payments. We can also accommodate specific billing requirements for enterprise clients.

We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees. All costs are clearly outlined in our proposals, including any setup fees, licensing costs, or third-party service charges. You'll know exactly what you're paying for before we begin any work.

Implementation

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Simple implementations may take 2-4 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions can take 3-12 months. We provide detailed project timelines during the planning phase and use agile methodologies to deliver value incrementally throughout the project.

We design our implementation processes to minimize business disruption. We typically work during off-hours for critical system changes, use phased rollouts, and maintain backup systems. Our goal is to ensure business continuity throughout the implementation process.

Yes, comprehensive training is included in all our implementations. We provide hands-on training sessions, documentation, video tutorials, and ongoing support to ensure your team can effectively use and manage the new systems. Training can be conducted on-site or remotely based on your preference.

We understand that requirements can evolve during a project. We use agile methodologies that accommodate changes and provide regular review points. Any scope changes are discussed, documented, and approved before implementation, with clear communication about timeline and cost implications.

Support & Maintenance

We provide comprehensive post-implementation support including 24/7 monitoring, regular maintenance, security updates, performance optimization, and technical support. Our support packages range from basic maintenance to full managed services, depending on your needs.

Our response times depend on the severity of the issue and your support package. Critical issues receive immediate attention (within 1 hour), high-priority issues within 4 hours, and standard requests within 24 hours. We provide 24/7 support for critical systems and emergencies.

Yes, we provide comprehensive remote support through secure connections. Our remote support capabilities include system monitoring, troubleshooting, maintenance, updates, and user assistance. We can resolve most issues remotely, reducing downtime and costs.

We provide comprehensive documentation including system architecture, user manuals, troubleshooting guides, and best practices. We also conduct knowledge transfer sessions to ensure your team understands the systems and can perform basic maintenance tasks independently.

Security & Compliance

We implement enterprise-grade security measures including encryption, access controls, regular security audits, and compliance with industry standards. All our team members sign NDAs, and we follow strict data handling procedures. We're certified in ISO 27001, SOC 2, and other security frameworks.

Yes, we help clients achieve and maintain compliance with various regulations including GDPR, HIPAA, SOX, PCI DSS, and industry-specific standards. Our solutions are designed with compliance in mind, and we provide ongoing compliance monitoring and reporting.

Yes, we conduct comprehensive security assessments including vulnerability scans, penetration testing, security audits, and risk assessments. We provide detailed reports with recommendations and help implement security improvements to protect your systems and data.

We have a comprehensive incident response plan that includes immediate containment, investigation, remediation, and recovery procedures. Our security operations center monitors systems 24/7, and we can respond to incidents within minutes. We also provide forensic analysis and help prevent future incidents.
